<div><h3>🌊 Essential Tips for New Survivors:</h3>
<ul><li><strong>Prioritize the Scanner:</strong> Craft the Scanner first – it unlocks blueprints and reveals resource locations.</li>
<li><strong>Stay near the Lifepod:</strong> Initial resources (titanium, copper, quartz) are abundant in the shallows.</li>
<li><strong>Watch your Oxygen:</strong> Never dive below 30 seconds of oxygen without an extra tank or nearby air source.</li>
<li><strong>Build a Habitat Builder ASAP:</strong> Allows you to build underwater bases with lockers, fabricators, and power sources.</li>
<li><strong>Use Beacons:</strong> Mark resource nodes, cave entrances, and interesting locations to avoid getting lost.</li></ul>
<h3>🏗️ Base Building Tips:</h3>
<ul><li>Start with a Multipurpose Room near a thermal vent or sunlight zone for easy power.</li>
<li>Place lockers against walls – use wall lockers to save space.</li>
<li>Grow plants indoors using plant pots – food becomes infinite.</li>
<li>Add a Scanner Room early – it reveals resource clusters and creature locations in a radius.</li></ul>
<h3>🐟 Creature Survival:</h3>
<ul><li>Avoid “Stalker” creatures – they are attracted to shiny objects (drop metal scrap to distract them).</li>
<li>Use the Repulsion Cannon or Perimeter Defense System for aggressive leviathans.</li>
<li>In co‑op, one player can lure creatures while the other collects resources.</li></ul>
